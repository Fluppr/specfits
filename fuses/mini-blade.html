Mini blade fuse (ATM)

The mini blade fuse is approximately half the height of the standard ATC fuse at 10.9 mm tall. It uses the same color-coded amperage system and is common in modern compact vehicles, motorcycles, and some late-model SUVs.

Specifications

PropertyValue
Standard designationATM / Mini ATC (SAE J1284)
Overall height10.9 mm
Width11.0 mm
Blade spacing3.6 mm
Voltage rating32 V DC
Amperage range2 A to 30 A

Color code

Mini blade fuses use the same color coding as standard ATC fuses. The amperage is also printed on the top of the plastic body.

ColorAmperage
Gray2 A
Violet3 A
Tan5 A
Brown7.5 A
Red10 A
Blue15 A
Yellow20 A
Green30 A

Are ATM and ATC interchangeable?

No. Mini blade fuses are physically smaller than standard ATC fuses — both in height and blade spacing (3.6 mm vs 5.08 mm). A mini blade fuse will rattle loosely and make no contact in a standard ATC holder. A standard ATC fuse will not fit in a mini blade holder at all. Always match the physical type exactly.

Common applications

  • Honda, Toyota, and other Asian brand vehicle fuse boxes (2000s onward)
  • Motorcycles and scooters
  • Compact European vehicles
  • Aftermarket accessories designed for tight space constraints
