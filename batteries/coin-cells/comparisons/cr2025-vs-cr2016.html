CR2025 vs CR2016

Both are 20mm 3V lithium cells, but CR2025 is 0.9mm thicker and typically offers longer runtime.

Side-by-side specs

SpecCR2025CR2016
Diameter20.0 mm20.0 mm
Thickness2.5 mm1.6 mm
Voltage3V3V
Typical capacity150-170 mAh75-95 mAh

View on Amazon | View on Amazon

Can you substitute one for the other?

Sometimes. CR2016 can be too thin for reliable contact where CR2025 is specified. CR2025 can be too thick for housings designed for CR2016.

Which devices take which

Compact fobs and thin housings may require CR2016. Many remotes and tags choose CR2025 for longer life.
