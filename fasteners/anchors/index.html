Wall anchors

Use a wall anchor when you need to fasten into a wall surface but there is no stud behind it, or when fastening into masonry or concrete. The correct anchor depends on wall material and load requirements.

Anchor typeBest forTypical load
Plastic expansion anchorsMasonry, concrete, tile10–50 lbs
Toggle boltsDrywall, plaster hollow walls50–300 lbs

General guidance

  • Always prefer fastening into a stud over using an anchor when loads exceed 20 lbs.
  • Anchor ratings are for shear (perpendicular to wall). Direct pull-out ratings are lower.
  • Never exceed the anchor's rated load. Use multiple anchors and spread the load for heavy items.
