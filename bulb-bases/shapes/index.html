Light bulb shape reference

Bulb shape codes describe the physical envelope of the glass — separate from the base type. A bulb is identified by both shape and base (e.g., A19/E26).

  • A19 — standard household bulb shape
  • A21 — larger household bulb shape
  • BR30 — bulged reflector flood
  • PAR38 — parabolic aluminized reflector spotlight
  • MR16 — multifaceted reflector
  • G25 — globe decorative shape
  • T8 — tubular fluorescent/LED shape
