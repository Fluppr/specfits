GU10 bulb base reference

GU10 is a twist-lock bi-pin spotlight base that runs directly on mains voltage (120V or 240V) with no transformer needed.

Exact specs

DesignationGU10 (twist-lock)
Pin diameter10 mm (center-to-center)
Pin tip diameter6.35 mm (mushroom-shaped)
VoltageMains (120V US / 240V EU) — no transformer
Typical wattage5W–50W LED/halogen equivalent

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

Recessed downlights, track lighting, and kitchen under-cabinet spotlights frequently use GU10 in both the US and Europe.

What it's interchangeable with

GU10 LED bulbs can directly replace GU10 halogen bulbs of the same form factor. No driver or transformer change is needed.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

GU10 and MR16 are different systems. MR16 uses 12V low voltage (via transformer), while GU10 uses mains voltage. Their pin shapes also differ — pins lock in GU10, push in MR16.

Common mistakes

  • Buying MR16 bulbs for GU10 fixtures — they will not fit or function.
  • Assuming a higher-wattage LED will be brighter — check lumens, not watts.
