LR44 vs SR44

They are usually the same size, but SR44 uses silver-oxide chemistry and tends to hold voltage more steadily.

Side-by-side specs

SpecLR44SR44
Diameter11.6 mm11.6 mm
Thickness5.4 mm5.4 mm
Voltage1.5V1.55V
ChemistryAlkalineSilver oxide
Drain behaviorDrops earlier under loadMore stable over discharge

Can you substitute one for the other?

Sometimes. Physical fit is usually fine, but precision devices may require SR44 voltage stability.

Which devices take which

General electronics may accept either. Watches, cameras, and meters often perform better with SR44.
