LR44 battery reference

An LR44 is a 1.5V alkaline button cell, typically 11.6mm wide and 5.4mm thick.

Exact specs

TypeLR44 (common aliases: AG13, A76)
Diameter11.6 mm
Thickness5.4 mm
Nominal voltage1.5V
ChemistryAlkaline manganese dioxide
Typical capacity110-150 mAh (drain dependent)

View on Amazon

What devices commonly use it

Small toys, laser pointers, calculators, and basic watches commonly use LR44-type cells.

What it's interchangeable with

LR44 and SR44 are often physically interchangeable. SR44 generally delivers flatter voltage under load and better high-drain performance.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

Do not replace with 3V coin cells such as CR2032 because voltage and form factor differ.

Common mistakes

  • Assuming all AG13-labeled cells are silver oxide.
  • Using LR44 in precision devices that expect SR44 stability.
  • Ignoring equivalent code confusion at checkout.
