CR2025 battery reference

A CR2025 is a 3V lithium coin cell that is 20mm wide and 2.5mm thick.

Exact specs

TypeCR2025
Diameter20.0 mm
Thickness2.5 mm
Nominal voltage3V
ChemistryLithium manganese dioxide
Typical capacity150-170 mAh

View on Amazon

What devices commonly use it

Slim remotes, key fobs, and compact sensors commonly use CR2025 where a thinner cell is required.

What it's interchangeable with

You can substitute CR2032 for CR2025 only if there is extra thickness clearance. CR2016 may work temporarily in some spring-contact trays but has lower capacity and can lose contact.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

Do not substitute with LR44/SR44 because those are 1.5V button cells with different diameter and chemistry behavior.

Common mistakes

  • Treating all 20mm coin cells as identical.
  • Forcing thicker cells into tight battery doors.
  • Ignoring intermittent contact from thinner substitutes.
