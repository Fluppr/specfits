E12 candelabra bulb base

E12 is the candelabra Edison screw base used primarily in the US, measuring 12mm in base diameter.

Exact specs

DesignationE12 (Candelabra)
Base diameter12 mm
Thread pitchEdison screw (E-series)
Primary regionNorth America
Typical wattage range5W–60W (incandescent equivalent)

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

Chandeliers, decorative sconces, nightlights, and ceiling fan light kits in North America commonly use E12 candelabra sockets.

What it's interchangeable with

E12 and E14 share the same general candelabra profile but differ by 2mm in diameter. An E14 bulb will not reliably thread into an E12 socket.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

E12 does not fit E26 or E27 standard sockets due to the large size difference.

Common mistakes

  • Purchasing E14 bulbs for US E12 chandeliers.
  • Using standard E26 bulbs in candelabra sockets.
  • Exceeding wattage limits on decorative fixtures.
