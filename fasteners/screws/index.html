Screw types

Screws are self-threading fasteners — they cut or form their own mating thread as they are driven. The correct screw type depends on the material being fastened and the load requirements.

TypeThreadTipPrimary material
Wood screwsCoarse, taperedSharp pointSoftwood, hardwood
Machine screwsFine, uniformBluntTapped metal, plastic
Drywall screwsCoarse or fineSharp pointGypsum board to wood or metal stud
