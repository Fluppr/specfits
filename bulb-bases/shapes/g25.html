G25 globe bulb shape

G25 is a round globe-shaped bulb, 25⁄8 inches (3.125 in / 79 mm) in diameter, commonly used in vanity and decorative applications.

Exact specs

Shape codeG25 (Globe)
Maximum diameter3.125 in (79 mm)
Common base pairingE26 (NA), E27 (EU)
Typical useBathroom vanity bar, pendant globe, exposed bulb fixtures

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

Vanity mirror bars, Hollywood-style bathroom fixtures, pendant lamps with open holders, and decorative exposed-bulb designs use G25 for its globe appearance.

What it's interchangeable with

G25 uses E26/E27 base, so any standard socket can electrically power it. However, its round shape may not fit in a shade designed for A19.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

G25 will not fit in shade fitters or enclosed globes designed for the narrower A19 profile.

Common mistakes

  • Buying G25 for a table lamp with a tight shade — the globe is too wide.
  • Using a G25 in a ceiling fan if the fan trim requires A19 profile.
