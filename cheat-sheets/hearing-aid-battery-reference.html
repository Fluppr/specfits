Hearing aid battery quick reference

All four standard hearing aid battery sizes at a glance. The tab color on the package and battery is the fastest way to match your device.

SizeTab colorIEC nameDiameterHeightCapacityTypical runtimeAid style
10YellowPR5365.8 mm3.6 mm~90 mAh3–7 daysIIC, CIC (smallest aids)
312BrownPR417.9 mm3.6 mm~175 mAh5–10 daysITC, RIC, RITE, mini BTE
13OrangePR487.9 mm5.4 mm~310 mAh10–14 daysBTE, full ITE
675BluePR4411.6 mm5.4 mm~600 mAh10–20 daysPower BTE, cochlear implant

Key rules

  • Color-coded packaging — yellow, brown, orange, and blue tabs are standardized across all brands (Rayovac, Energizer, Duracell, Phonak, etc.).
  • Wait 1 minute after removing the tab before inserting the battery — this activates the zinc-air chemistry fully and extends runtime.
  • 10 and 312 look identical in diameter (both 7.9 mm for 312; 5.8 mm for 10) — always check the number not just the size by feel.
  • 312 and 13 share the same 7.9 mm diameter but different heights — they are not interchangeable.
  • Open the battery door at night to extend battery life — prevents unnecessary drain.

Interchangeability summary

PairSame voltage?Interchangeable?
10 ↔ 312Yes (1.4V)No — different diameter (5.8 vs 7.9 mm)
312 ↔ 13Yes (1.4V)No — different height (3.6 vs 5.4 mm)
13 ↔ 675Yes (1.4V)No — different diameter (7.9 vs 11.6 mm)
