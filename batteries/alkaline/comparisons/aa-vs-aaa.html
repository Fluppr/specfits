AA vs AAA battery

Both are 1.5V and use the same chemistry, but AA is wider and longer with roughly 2x the capacity of AAA.

Side-by-side specs

SpecAAAAA
Diameter14.5 mm10.5 mm
Length50.5 mm44.5 mm
Voltage1.5V1.5V
Typical capacity2400–3000 mAh860–1200 mAh

Can you substitute one for the other?

No. The diameter difference (4mm) means neither format physically fits in the other's tray. This is not a spring-clearance situation — the housing sizes are incompatible.

Which devices take which

TV remotes, mice, and flashlights usually take AA for longer runtime. Slim remotes, small flashlights, and portable keyboards more often require AAA for their narrower profile.
