MR16 bulb base reference

MR16 is a 12V low-voltage bi-pin spotlight that requires a transformer or LED driver — it is not a mains-voltage base like GU10.

Exact specs

DesignationMR16 (GU5.3 pin base)
Pin spacing5.3 mm (GU5.3)
Voltage12V AC/DC (requires transformer)
Reflector diameter2 inches (50 mm)
Typical wattage5W–50W LED/halogen equivalent

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

MR16 is found in track lighting systems with a 12V transformer, recessed low-voltage downlights, and landscape lighting systems.

What it's interchangeable with

MR16 LED bulbs replace MR16 halogen bulbs in existing 12V transformer setups. Some LED MR16s work with dimmable LED drivers; confirm compatibility before purchasing.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

MR16 is not interchangeable with GU10. GU10 runs on mains voltage and has a different pin lock mechanism. Installing an MR16 bulb in a GU10 socket will not work and may damage the bulb.

Common mistakes

  • Buying GU10 bulbs for an MR16 track system — the pins will not engage.
  • Assuming LED MR16 is compatible with an old magnetic transformer — check for an electronic driver instead.
