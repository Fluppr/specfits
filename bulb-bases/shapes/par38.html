PAR38 bulb shape

PAR38 is a parabolic aluminized reflector spotlight, 4.75 inches (121 mm) in maximum diameter, used for focused outdoor and commercial lighting.

Exact specs

Shape codePAR38
Maximum diameter4.75 in (121 mm)
Length (approximate)5.5 in (140 mm)
Common base pairingE26 (standard) or E39 (mogul, commercial)
Beam typeSpot or flood (10°–40°)
Typical fixtureOutdoor floodlights, commercial downlights, large track

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

Outdoor security lights, commercial track lighting, and large retail display fixtures commonly use PAR38.

What it's interchangeable with

PAR38 LED replaces PAR38 halogen or incandescent directly. In 6-inch cans that accept PAR38, a BR30 can also fit but with a different beam angle.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

PAR38 is too large for most residential 4-inch can fixtures designed for BR30 or smaller reflector bulbs.

Common mistakes

  • Ordering PAR38 for a 4-inch can when BR30 is the correct fit.
  • Using an indoor PAR38 in outdoor fixtures without confirming it is rated for wet/damp conditions.
