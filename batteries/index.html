Coin cell batteries

Lithium and alkaline button cells — CR, LR, and SR chemistry. Match diameter and height exactly; never substitute a thicker cell.

  • CR2032 — 20 mm, 3.2 mm
  • CR2025 — 20 mm, 2.5 mm
  • CR2016 — 20 mm, 1.6 mm
  • LR44 / AG13 alkaline
  • SR44 silver oxide

Hearing aid batteries

Zinc-air button cells identified by color-coded tab. The tab seals the air holes — remove it just before use, not in advance.

  • Size 10 — yellow tab
  • Size 312 — brown tab
  • Size 13 — orange tab
  • Size 675 — blue tab
