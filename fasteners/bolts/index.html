Bolt types

Bolts require a nut or a tapped hole. Unlike wood screws, they rely on clamping force rather than thread engagement in a soft material for their holding strength.

TypeHead styleUse case
Hex boltsHexagonal (wrench-driven)Structural joints, machinery, general assembly
Carriage boltsRound dome (no drive)Wood-to-wood, deck framing, furniture
