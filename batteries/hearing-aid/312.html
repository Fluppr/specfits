Size 312 hearing aid battery

The most widely used hearing aid battery worldwide. Brown tab, 7.9 mm diameter, 3.6 mm height, ~175 mAh capacity. IEC code: PR41.

Specifications

PropertyValue
IEC designationPR41
Tab colorBrown
ChemistryZinc-air
Nominal voltage1.45 V
Diameter7.9 mm
Height3.6 mm
Typical capacity~175 mAh
Typical runtime3–10 days depending on aid style and usage
Shelf life (with tab)3–4 years

Compatible devices

  • Receiver-in-canal (RIC/RITE) hearing aids — the most common modern style
  • Mini behind-the-ear (mini BTE) aids
  • Some in-the-canal (ITC) aids

How to identify it

Look for the brown plastic tab covering air holes on the flat face. The size "312" or "PR41" may be printed on the battery face. It is the same diameter as the Size 13 but noticeably thinner (3.6 mm vs 5.4 mm).

Activation

Peel the brown tab fully and wait 60–120 seconds before inserting into the hearing aid. The rest period allows oxygen to saturate the zinc electrode, which measurably improves initial performance and total runtime.

Common mistakes

  • Inserting immediately after removing the tab — skipping the rest period reduces run time by up to 20%.
  • Confusing 312 with Size 13 — both are 7.9 mm wide. Check the height: 312 is thinner. See the 312 vs 13 comparison.
  • Storing with tab removed — once exposed to air, the battery is live and will drain within days.
