Fuse lookup

Type a fuse code, type, color, or amperage. Results update as you type.

Where to find the code: The code is printed on the fuse body (e.g., ATC15, ATM10) or stamped on the metal terminals. If the print is worn, the color of the fuse body tells you the amperage — see the fuse color codes chart. The vehicle owner’s manual also lists each fuse slot, rating, and circuit.
Safety: Always replace with the exact same amperage. Installing a higher-rated fuse will not protect the circuit and may cause wiring damage or fire.
