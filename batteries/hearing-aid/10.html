Size 10 hearing aid battery

The smallest standard hearing aid battery. Yellow tab, 5.8 mm diameter, 3.6 mm height, ~90 mAh. Used in the smallest invisible and in-the-canal aids. IEC code: PR536.

Specifications

PropertyValue
IEC designationPR536
Tab colorYellow
ChemistryZinc-air
Nominal voltage1.45 V
Diameter5.8 mm
Height3.6 mm
Typical capacity~90 mAh
Typical runtime3–7 days
Shelf life (with tab)3–4 years

Compatible devices

  • Invisible-in-canal (IIC) hearing aids — the smallest style
  • Completely-in-canal (CIC) aids
  • In-the-canal (ITC) aids with very small battery doors

How to identify it

The yellow tab identifies Size 10. It is the smallest of the four standard sizes at 5.8 mm in diameter — noticeably smaller than Size 312 (7.9 mm) when placed side by side. The size "10A" or "PR536" may be printed on the face.

Runtime management

At ~90 mAh, Size 10 batteries deplete faster than other hearing aid sizes. Users of IIC/CIC aids should plan on replacing batteries every 3–5 days with typical use. Higher-power features like Bluetooth streaming drain Size 10 significantly faster — sometimes within 2 days.

Activation

Peel the yellow tab and wait 60 seconds before inserting. The rest period is proportionally more important for Size 10 since its smaller capacity means any efficiency loss is more noticeable.
