Size 675 hearing aid battery

The largest and highest-capacity hearing aid battery. Blue tab, 11.6 mm diameter, 5.4 mm height, ~600 mAh. Used in power BTE aids and cochlear implant sound processors. IEC code: PR44.

Specifications

PropertyValue
IEC designationPR44
Tab colorBlue
ChemistryZinc-air
Nominal voltage1.45 V
Diameter11.6 mm
Height5.4 mm
Typical capacity~600 mAh
Typical runtime9–20 days
Shelf life (with tab)3–4 years

Compatible devices

  • Power behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids — for severe to profound hearing loss
  • Cochlear implant sound processors (most brands)
  • Some bone-anchored hearing aids (BAHA)

How to identify it

The blue tab and notably larger diameter (11.6 mm vs 7.9 mm for sizes 312 and 13) makes the 675 easy to identify by sight. It is visibly the largest of the four standard hearing aid sizes.

Activation

Peel the blue tab and wait at least 60 seconds — preferably 2 minutes — before inserting. The larger electrode volume benefits more from the full rest period than smaller sizes do.

Common mistakes

  • Using a Size 13 instead — same height but smaller diameter (7.9 mm vs 11.6 mm). Will not seat properly and may rattle in the compartment.
  • Storing multiple batteries loose together — tabs can catch on each other and be partially lifted, activating the cells.
