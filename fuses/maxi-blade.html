Maxi blade fuse (APX)

The maxi blade is the largest of the standard automotive blade fuse family. At 29.2 mm tall, it handles high-current circuits from 20 A to 100 A and is found in under-hood fuse/relay boxes for circuits like the alternator, main power feed, and ABS.

Common applications

  • Main battery feed circuits in passenger vehicles
  • Alternator output protection
  • ABS and stability control system feeds
  • High-current accessories: winches, inverters, air compressors
  • Under-hood relay and fuse boxes

Replacement considerations

Maxi fuses are typically accessible in an under-hood main fuse box rather than the cabin fuse panel. Always disconnect the negative battery terminal before replacing a maxi fuse to avoid arc flash from high-current circuits. Replace only with the matching amperage and a genuine-specification replacement.
