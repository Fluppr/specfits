E26 vs E27

E26 (US) and E27 (EU) are nearly identical in thread dimensions — 1mm separates them — and most bulbs physically fit both sockets. Voltage is the critical variable, not the base.

Can you substitute one for the other?

Physically: usually yes. Electrically: only if voltage matches. An E26 bulb rated 120V will not produce full output or may fail in a 240V E27 socket. Always match bulb voltage to mains voltage.

Which devices take which

US fixtures use E26. European fixtures use E27. Globally distributed LED brands often specify both on the packaging.
