CR2016 battery reference

A CR2016 is a 3V lithium coin cell that is 20mm wide and 1.6mm thick.

Exact specs

TypeCR2016
Diameter20.0 mm
Thickness1.6 mm
Nominal voltage3V
ChemistryLithium manganese dioxide
Typical capacity75-95 mAh

View on Amazon

What devices commonly use it

Slim key fobs, compact fitness gadgets, and small remote controls may be designed around CR2016 thickness.

What it's interchangeable with

CR2025 or CR2032 should only replace CR2016 when the compartment can physically close and maintain contact pressure. Thicker cells can damage battery doors in tight housings.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

It is not interchangeable with LR44/SR44 button cells due to voltage, shape, and chemistry differences.

Common mistakes

  • Using CR2016 as a drop-in for CR2025 without checking contact reliability.
  • Assuming same diameter means same runtime.
  • Overlooking low-capacity effects in high-drain devices.
