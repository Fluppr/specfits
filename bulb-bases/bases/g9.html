G9 bulb base reference

G9 is a mains-voltage loop-pin capsule base, recognizable by its two small loop-shaped pins spaced 9mm apart.

Exact specs

DesignationG9 (loop bi-pin)
Pin spacing9 mm (center-to-center)
Pin formLooped / folded wire
VoltageMains (120V / 240V) — no transformer needed
Typical wattage2W–25W LED equivalent

View on Amazon

What fixtures commonly use it

G9 is common in compact pendant lamps, wall sconces, bathroom vanity bars, and decorative table lamps — particularly in European-style fixtures.

What it's interchangeable with

G9 LED capsules replace G9 halogen capsules directly with no transformer change. Confirm lamp wattage and color temperature match your preference.

What it's NOT interchangeable with

G9 is not compatible with G4 (different pin spacing, low-voltage), GU10 (mains twist-lock), or any screw base.

Common mistakes

  • Confusing G9 with G4 — they visually appear similar but G4 runs on 12V.
  • Touching G9 halogen bulbs with bare hands — oils reduce lifespan.
