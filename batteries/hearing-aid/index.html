Hearing aid batteries

Hearing aid batteries are zinc-air cells — activated by removing a colored tab that exposes air holes. There are four standard sizes, each assigned a universal color code by IEC 60086-3.

SizeTab colorDiameterHeightCapacityTypical aid style
Size 10Yellow5.8 mm3.6 mm~90 mAhITC, CIC, IIC
Size 312Brown7.9 mm3.6 mm~175 mAhRIC, RITE, mini BTE
Size 13Orange7.9 mm5.4 mm~310 mAhBTE, full-shell ITE
Size 675Blue11.6 mm5.4 mm~600 mAhPower BTE, cochlear implant

Comparisons

  • Size 312 vs Size 13
  • Size 675 vs Size 312

Key notes for all zinc-air batteries

  • Remove the tab and wait 1–2 minutes before inserting — this activates the cell and extends runtime.
  • Never peel the tab until ready to use. Shelf life once removed is measured in days, not months.
  • Sealed in packaging, shelf life is 3–4 years from manufacture date.
  • All four sizes share the same 1.45V nominal voltage.
  • Do not use in sealed or airtight environments — they require oxygen to function.
